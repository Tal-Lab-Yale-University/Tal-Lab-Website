Pranav graduated with a BS in Computer Science and Mathematics from
Dickinson College in May 2025. His undergraduate research involved
understanding the gene expression profile of the PMA response in the AML
cell line HL-60.

He also worked on extending and improving a submodular feature-based
algorithm to select subsets of scRNA-seq atlases while preserving the
transcriptional heterogeneity within the data. In the Tal Lab, Pranav
hopes to expand his understanding of postpartum endometrial remodeling
and computational techniques for analyzing bulk and single-cell genomic,
epigenomic, proteomic, and transcriptomic datasets, among others,
before attending graduate school.

In his free time, Pranav likes to play pickleball, dance salsa, and
play hard rock solos on his electric guitar.
